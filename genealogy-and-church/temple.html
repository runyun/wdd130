A way back to God

Taiwan Taipei Temple

In the temple, we can do ordinances that help people who pass away. The purpose of these ordinances is to help people return to God’s presence and be with family after life through God’s power. Those people who pass away without the knowledge of God will have a chance to be taught after their death.

Kansas City Temple Baptismal Font

We can help our ancestors to do the ordinances which include baptisms and others ordinances in the temple. Our ancestors can decide whether to receive these offers or not.
